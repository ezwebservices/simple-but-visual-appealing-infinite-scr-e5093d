# Financial Plan: KidScroll Math

**Product:** Infinite-scroll math learning app for ages 3-5 — TikTok-style lessons with animal characters, audio playback, counting/addition/subtraction practice.

---

## 1. Pricing Strategy

### Model: Freemium + Subscription

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | 5 lessons/day, 2 animal characters, basic counting (1-10) |
| **Monthly** | $4.99/mo | Unlimited lessons, all animal characters, addition & subtraction, progress tracking, offline mode |
| **Annual** | $39.99/yr (~$3.33/mo) | Everything in Monthly + family sharing (up to 3 kids), bonus seasonal content packs |
| **Lifetime** | $79.99 one-time | Full access forever, all future content included |

### Why This Pricing Works
- **$4.99 impulse-buy threshold** — parents don't overthink it
- **Annual discount (33% off)** drives retention and upfront cash
- **Lifetime tier** appeals to deal-seekers and funds early development
- **No ads ever** — parents pay to avoid ads for their kids; this is a selling point
- **Free tier is generous enough** to hook the child before the parent converts

### Competitor Benchmarks
| App | Price |
|-----|-------|
| Khan Academy Kids | Free |
| Moose Math | Free (limited) |
| Todo Math | $7.99/mo |
| Endless Numbers | $8.99 one-time |
| Homer | $9.99/mo |

Our pricing undercuts premium competitors while offering a more engaging format than free alternatives.

- **Churn below 8%** (driven by fresh content + child engagement)

---

## 6. Key Financial Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Low conversion (< 4%) | Revenue 35% below projections | A/B test paywall timing; let kids get hooked before gate |
| High churn (> 12%) | Subscriber base stalls | Weekly new content drops; push notifications to parents |
| COPPA/privacy compliance costs | $2K-5K legal + technical | Build privacy-first from day 1; no data collection on kids |
| Apple/Google policy changes | Revenue model disruption | Build direct web subscription as backup |
| Competition from free apps | Slower adoption | Differentiate on scroll UX — no one else does TikTok-for-math |

## 7. Funding Strategy

### Bootstrap Path (Recommended for MVP)
- **Personal investment:** $10,000-15,000
- **Revenue-funded growth** after Month 6

### If Seeking Investment
- **Pre-seed round:** $50,000-100,000
- **Use of funds:** Accelerate content library, hire part-time animator, expand marketing
- **Target:** 10,000 paid subscribers within 12 months → $50K+ ARR → position for seed round
