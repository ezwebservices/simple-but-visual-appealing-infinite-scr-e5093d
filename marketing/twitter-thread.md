## Tweet 5 (Sound Design)

At age 4, sound IS the interface.

Every number is spoken aloud
Every tap: pop, boing, or squeak
Correct answer: ascending chime + character celebration
Wrong answer: gentle "boop" + "Almost! Try again!"

Background: soft xylophone melodies in major key

We designed ears-first, eyes-second.

## Tweet 8 (Design Details)

Design decisions that matter when your user is 4:

- 64px+ touch targets (tiny fingers need big buttons)
- Full-screen scenes (no nav bars, no chrome)
- Rounded, chunky shapes everywhere
- Bubbly typography (Fredoka One / Baloo 2)
- Numbers: extra bold with subtle bounce shadow
