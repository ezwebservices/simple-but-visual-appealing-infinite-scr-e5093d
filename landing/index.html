What Makes NumPals Smart

An adaptive engine that meets each child where they are — and grows with them.

Adaptive Mastery Algorithm

17 concepts from counting to 5 through subtraction to 10. The app tracks streaks and accuracy, automatically advancing your child when they're ready.

Multi-Child Profiles

Add multiple children, each with their own avatar, progress, and mastery level. Switch between kids with one tap.

Parent Dashboard

PIN-protected dashboard shows a concept mastery matrix across all 17 skills, accuracy trends over time, and recent activity — per child.

Sound-First, Voice Narrated

Every number, prompt, and explanation is spoken aloud via Web Speech API. No reading required — your child hears everything.

Quick Lessons on Mistakes

Wrong answer? A quick lesson overlay explains the concept with visuals and narration before moving on. Mistakes become teaching moments.

Safe & Ad-Free

No ads, no in-app purchases, no external links, no data collection. A completely contained, safe experience for your child.

How NumPals Works

Three simple steps. Zero setup. Instant learning.

1

Add Your Child

Create a profile, pick an avatar from 6 characters, and your child starts at the beginning — counting to 5.

2

Swipe Through Lessons

Each swipe is a bite-sized lesson matched to their current level. The algorithm auto-advances as they master each concept.

3

Track Their Mastery

Open the parent dashboard to see exactly which of the 17 concepts they've mastered, what they're working on, and accuracy trends.

Built for Parents

Real tools to track your child's learning — not just a game you hand over and hope for the best.

PIN-Protected Dashboard

Set a 4-digit PIN so only you can access the parent dashboard. Your child stays in the learning zone while you manage profiles and review progress.

Per-Child Progress Tracking

Each child has their own accuracy sparkline charts that show trends over time. See exactly how each kid is progressing day by day.

Concept Mastery Matrix

A visual grid of all 17 concepts — grouped by skill area — showing locked, active, practicing, and mastered status at a glance. Know exactly where your child is.
