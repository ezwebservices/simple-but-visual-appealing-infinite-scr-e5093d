# NumPals Brand Guide

## Name

**NumPals** — Numbers + Pals (animal friends)

## Tagline

**"Swipe, Count, Roar!"**

## Pitch

NumPals is a TikTok-style infinite scroll math app for preschoolers (ages 3-5). Each swipe reveals a bite-sized lesson starring lovable animal characters who teach counting, addition, and subtraction through animated visuals, playful sounds, and tap-to-interact moments. No reading required — every interaction is visual, auditory, and tactile. Kids don't study math; they play with their animal pals and math just happens.

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute       | Description                                                                 |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Playful**     | Everything sounds like a game. "Tap the frogs!" not "Solve 2 + 3."         |
| **Encouraging** | Celebrate every attempt. Wrong answers get "Almost! Try again!" not errors. |
| **Simple**      | One-word labels, big icons, zero jargon. A 4-year-old navigates alone.     |
| **Warm**        | Like a favorite stuffed animal — safe, familiar, cozy.                     |
| **Silly**       | Animal sounds, wobbly animations, surprise confetti. Joy is the metric.    |

### Language Rules

- Max 3-4 words per on-screen label
- Use animal names as verbs: "Let's panda-count!" / "Frog-jump to five!"
- Numbers are always shown AND spoken aloud
- No negative feedback — only encouragement and gentle redirection
- Sound effects on every tap (pop, boing, squeak)

## Target Audience

### Primary: Children aged 3-5

- Pre-readers — all UI must work without text comprehension
- Short attention spans — lessons are 10-15 seconds per card
- Motivated by color, sound, movement, and animal characters
- Learning to count 1-20, intro to addition/subtraction (sums to 10)

### Secondary: Parents & Caregivers

- Want screen time that feels educational
- Value no-ads, safe, self-contained experiences
- Appreciate progress tracking (optional parent dashboard)
- Trust cute, polished design over flashy gimmicks

## Design Principles

1. **Big & Tappable** — Minimum touch target 64x64px. Chunky rounded shapes.
2. **Full-Screen Visual** — Every card is a complete scene, no chrome or nav clutter.
3. **Sound-First** — Audio narrates everything. Visuals reinforce, not replace.
4. **Scroll = Progress** — Infinite scroll creates flow state. No menus, no friction.
5. **Celebrate Everything** — Stars, confetti, bouncing animals on every correct answer.
6. **Safe & Contained** — No external links, no ads, no text input, no social features.

## Typography

- **Display/Headers:** Rounded, bubbly sans-serif (e.g., Baloo 2, Fredoka One)
- **Numbers:** Extra bold, slightly bouncy with subtle shadow
- **Body (parent UI only):** Clean sans-serif (e.g., Nunito, Quicksand)

## Audio Identity

- **Background:** Soft, looping xylophone/marimba melodies (major key, 90-110 BPM)
- **Correct answer:** Ascending chime + character celebration sound
- **Wrong answer:** Gentle "boop" + encouraging voice line
- **Swipe transition:** Soft whoosh
- **Character voices:** Warm, gender-neutral, slightly high-pitched (think animated show narrator)
